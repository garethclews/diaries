About Us

What this is

Welcome to The Fishman Diaries. A newsletter for my local statistics and machine learning community of interest. There are much, much better newsletters and points of interest than this one - and very frequently the content overlaps. Where I use the content from those I am subscribed to I use the utm info to respect the redirect.

Better alternatives include (but are not limited to) The Data Science Roundup and Data Science Weekly

Opinionated

Our origin story

Whilst attempting to decide on a name for our little group there was an online straw poll. One response came from fish_man_168 and the name stuck. Note that no individual is Fishman and that he is instead the mascot of our community, although at times where we purport to be this is simply for effect.

Our logo is not our own, it is the Flying Hellfish logo from The Simpsons.
